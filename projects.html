Fake Job Detection System

Developed a machine learning-based application to identify and classify fraudulent job postings. The system analyzes job descriptions and recruitment-related data to distinguish between genuine and fake job opportunities.

Implemented data preprocessing, feature extraction, and predictive modeling techniques to improve detection accuracy. This project helps job seekers avoid scams and make informed career decisions.
